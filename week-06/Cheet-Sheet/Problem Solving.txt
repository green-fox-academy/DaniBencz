
Problem Solving

1, Understand
	- can you explain it in plain English?
	- write it down, or doodle it

2, Plan
	- give the brain time

3, Divide
	- break it down to steps you can handle (reduce)
	- solve sub-problems
	- connect the dots

4, Stuck?
	- stay curious, not irritated
	- debug: step by step
	- change pesrpective
	- start fresh anew
	- research

5, Practise
